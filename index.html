Our Services

Interior Decorating

Transform your space with our full-service interior decorating. From concept to completion, we curate color palettes, furniture layouts, and décor elements that bring elegance and functionality to every room. Whether you're refreshing a single space or reimagining your entire home, we'll make it beautifully you.

Consulting

Not ready for a full redesign but still need a professional touch? Our interior design consulting service offers personalized, professional advice to help you make confident decisions. Whether you're choosing paint colors, arranging furniture, or developing a cohesive style, our 20+ years of experience will offer you designs that elevate your space.

Fixture and Furniture Sourcing

Need help finding the perfect finishing touches for your space? With access to trusted vendors and a sharp eye for design, we source fixtures and furnishings that reflect your style and elevate your home. From standout pieces to functional staples, we make the process seamless and stress-free.

About Me

I have been an interior decorator for over 23 years. My passion for transformation began early, driven by the joy of choosing the perfect furnishings for my apartments and homes over the years. Each selection allowed me to express my personality and create inviting spaces. I attended a design trade school, successfully completing programs in interior decorating before starting my own business. My design work has taken me to various cities and states across the country, including Las Vegas, San Francisco, Austin, Boise, and more.

I provide a wide range of interior decorating services tailored to meet various client needs. I offer color consultation, design and installation of custom window coverings and bedding, sourcing furniture and fixtures, utilizing AutoCAD for space planning, complete bathroom and kitchen remodels, and comprehensive project management.

For those looking to transform their homes, I focus on decorative solutions that elevate the aesthetic, while also improving the practicality and efficiency of your home. I oversee every aspect of the design process to ensure a seamless execution and a stress-free experience for my clients.
